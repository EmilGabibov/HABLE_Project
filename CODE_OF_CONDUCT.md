# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of background or experience.

Contributors are expected to be respectful and constructive, assume good intent during technical disagreements, focus feedback on the work, and protect private information.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized comments, doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when necessary to keep the project safe.
